Gabber's Album Log

Albums I've heard and my feelings about them!

The Spark- Enter Shikari

A beautiful blend of synths and orchestra, which as shikari aims to do, manipulates my emotions every time, special shoutout to Live Outside!

Tekkno- Electric Callboy

A no- skip album that makes me "hypa" every time I listen to it.

Acts of Fear and Love- Soft Play

An album to remind me of discovering Southampton- the start of the rest of my life. An amazing neo-punk outlook on every day life.
